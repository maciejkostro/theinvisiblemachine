# Dictionary

## A

### Authentication
A procedure used to verify the identity of a payment service user or the valid use of a payment instrument.

### Authorised payment transaction
A payment transaction for which the payer has given consent.

### Account servicing payment service provider (ASPSP)
A payment service provider that provides and maintains a payment account for a payer.

### Acquirer
A payment service provider that enables a payee to accept and process card-based payment transactions.

### Account information service
An online service providing consolidated information on one or more payment accounts held with one or more providers.

### Anti-money laundering
The legal and supervisory framework aimed at preventing the use of the financial system for money laundering and related illicit purposes.

### Automated individual decision-making
A decision based solely on automated processing that produces legal or similarly significant effects for a person.

## C

### Credit transfer
A payment service that credits the payee’s account on the basis of an instruction given by the payer.

### Card-based payment transaction
A payment transaction made through a payment card scheme’s infrastructure and rules, including use through digital or IT devices where it results in a card transaction.

### Customer due diligence
Measures used to identify and verify customers and assess relevant risk for AML purposes.

### Creditworthiness assessment
The assessment of whether a consumer is likely to meet obligations under a credit agreement.

### Crypto-asset
A digital representation of a value or a right that can be transferred and stored electronically using distributed ledger technology or similar technology.

## D

### Digital operational resilience
The ability of a financial entity to build, assure, and review its ICT operational integrity and reliability.

### Digital euro
A proposed form of retail central bank digital money for use in the euro area.

## E

### Electronic identification
The use of electronic identification means and identity data to identify a natural or legal person.

### European Digital Identity Wallet
A wallet framework under the European Digital Identity regime for storing and presenting identity data and attestations electronically.

## F

### Four-party payment card scheme
A card scheme in which issuer and acquirer are separate and the transaction runs through the scheme between payer and payee accounts.

## I

### Instant credit transfer
A euro credit transfer designed to make funds available to the payee within seconds, at any time.

### Issuer
A payment service provider that provides a payer with a payment instrument for card-based payment transactions.

### Interchange fee
A fee paid for each card transaction between issuer and acquirer, directly or indirectly.

### Instant payments
Payments executed in real time or near real time, without traditional delay buffers.

## N

### NFC
A short-range wireless communication technology used for close-proximity device interaction, including contactless payments.

## O

### Operational resilience
The ability of a financial entity or system to continue delivering critical services through disruption.

## P

### Payment system
A funds transfer system with formal and standardised arrangements for processing, clearing, or settling payment transactions.

### Payment service provider (PSP)
An entity that provides payment services under PSD2 or benefits from a relevant exemption.

### Payment account
An account used for the execution of payment transactions.

### Payment instrument
A personalised device or set of procedures used to initiate a payment order.

### Payer
The person who gives a payment order or, where relevant, holds the account from which funds are sent.

### Payee
The intended recipient of funds in a payment transaction.

### Payment card scheme
A set of rules, practices, standards, or guidelines for executing card-based payment transactions.

### Payment initiation service
A service that initiates a payment order at the request of the payment service user in relation to a payment account held with another provider.

### Personal data
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.

### Processing of personal data
Any operation performed on personal data, whether automated or not.

## S

### Strong customer authentication (SCA)
Authentication based on at least two independent elements from knowledge, possession, and inherence.

### SEPA
The common euro payments area in which shared technical and business requirements apply to euro transfers and direct debits.

### SEPA Instant Credit Transfer (SCT Inst)
The EPC scheme for euro instant credit transfers.

### Settlement finality
The legal protection of transfer orders and netting from being unwound once the system rules and legal conditions are met.

## T

### Tokenisation
The replacement of a card’s primary account number with a token used in payment processing.

### Trust service
An electronic service that supports electronic signatures, seals, timestamps, delivery, website authentication, or related trust functions.

## V

### Verification of Payee (VoP)
A pre-execution check of payee-related data before a credit transfer is sent.
